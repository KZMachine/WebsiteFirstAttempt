Technical Interests
 My two biggest interests within the field of Computer Science would be Artificial Intelligence and Robotics. Growing up, I was always interested in both of these fields, however, my fascination did not pique until 2018, when swept by my hometown, Katy, Texas, and destroyed hundreds of homes, including my own. In the weeks following the disaster, I watched as drones were deployed to search and rescue people that were stranded in their homes. Seeing just how useful the drones were made me realize that I wanted to go into the field of robotics. In addition, I had read about the multitude of benefits of applying AI to robotics in multiple articles, so I wanted to help the combination of these fields to progress much faster than they already do.
